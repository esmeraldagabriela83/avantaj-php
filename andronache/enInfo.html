A positive experience !

Dentistry is the evaluation, diagnosis, prevention and treatment of diseases related to the oral cavity and the maxillo-facial region.
The dental office offers from consultation and routine control to complex restorative prosthetic treatments.
With a strong focus on oral health and hygiene, general dentistry helps people avoid the progression of oral disease.
If left untreated, they result in pain, lost time at work or school, and a high cost each year.

Who is the one healthcare provider you see more than any other??
For many people, the answer is simple: the dentist.

There is a good reason for this - this constitutes the fact that general dentistry is uniquely oriented towards disease prevention. In fact, it is estimated that over 50% of all dental procedures are either diagnostic or preventative.

A better life begins with a beautiful smile !

General dentistry services are the first reason why patients go to the dental office.
General dentistry is important for maintaining the health of a beautiful smile as well as general health.
Ignoring dental caries and periodontal disease can cause local infections and general diseases at a distance with multiple manifestations throughout the body ( outbreak disease ) .

Unfortunately, in Romania, patients, regardless of social category, present themselves to the dentist as an emergency with pain and dental abscess.
This leads to tooth devitalization (nerve removal, endodontic treatment and odontal treatment) or tooth extraction.

Losing teeth is not a solution .

The natural smile offers the best business card and healthy teeth guarantee the best efficiency in the mastication process.

General dental services are important for the prevention and early detection of oral pathology as well as the detection of general diseases with local manifestations in the oral cavity.

Prevention is cheaper than treatment.

We offer general dentistry services for patients of all ages, for all family members. It is recommended that everyone benefit twice a year from consultation, control, sanitation.
